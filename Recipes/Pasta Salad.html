Pasta Salad

A pasta salad is a refreshing and versatile dish that combines cooked pasta with an array of vibrant ingredients. It is typically served chilled, making it perfect for warm weather or as a side dish for barbecues and picnics. The pasta acts as a sturdy base, providing a satisfying texture. Alongside the pasta, you'll find a colorful medley of fresh vegetables like cherry tomatoes, crisp cucumbers, and crunchy bell peppers. These vegetables add a burst of freshness and contribute to the salad's visual appeal. Additionally, pasta salads often include other flavorful elements such as tangy olives, zesty herbs like basil or parsley, and a light dressing that ties all the ingredients together. The result is a delightful combination of flavors and textures, making pasta salad a versatile and delicious option for any occasion.

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Dressing
  • Seasoning mix
  • Vegetables
  1. Gather all your ingredients.
  2. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Cook pasta in the boiling water, stirring occasionally, until tender yet firm to the bite, about 10 to 12 minutes; rinse under cold water and drain.
  3. Whisk Italian dressing and salad spice mix together until smooth. Combine pasta, tomatoes, bell peppers, and olives in a salad bowl.
  4. Pour dressing over salad and toss to coat.
  5. Refrigerate salad, 8 hours to overnight.
  6. Enjoy!
